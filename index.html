Emma Charlotte Watson

English actress, model and activist

Emma Watson Overview

Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson was born on April 15th, 1990 in Paris, France. At a young age she attended the Stagecoach Theatre Arts school at Oxford. Her natural instinct for acting was noticed when she won a poetry contest for reciting "The Sea" by James Reeves at the age of seven. She is best known for her role as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter series. Throughout the series, her character uses her cleverness and logical thinking to help her friends defeat Lord Vordermort also referred to as the "Dark Lord". Harry Potter was not the only film series she was brilliant in, as she has acted in other multiple films including Perks of being a Wallflower, Beauty and the Beast, and Little Women to name a few.

Outside of the Big Screen

Emma Watson has also emerged as a fashionista, with her style catching the eyes of many major figures in the fashion industry. In September of 2009 she announced that she was working with People Tree, a fashion label that promotes fair trade. In the same year she managed to excel into the world of fashion and modeling, as she was chosen to be the face of Burberry's Autumn/Winter collection, and again in 2010 for the Spring/Summer collection. Maintaining her commitment to her education, she enrolled at Brown University in the Fall of 2009. Sticking to it, she graduated with her Bachelors in 2014 with an English Degree. Following her graduation in the same year she was appointed a UN Women Goodwill Ambassador.
